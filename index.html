Top 5 meaningful family anime movies - Animepahe

Japanese anime movies always give us many different emotions, especially family-themed films at Animepahe. In this article, I would like to introduce to you the 5 best Japanese anime about family.

1. Doraemon

Doraemon is a famous anime film, the movie is about the love in the family between parents and sons. The film opens with a robot cat from the future Doraemon sent by Sewashi - Nobita's five-generation grandson to the 21st century to help his grandfather.

Nobita was confused and began to get familiar with this robotic cat. Both have created a funny situation, creating joy for the audience. Will Doraemon with his magical treasures, will do to help Nobita overcome the trouble he caused himself?

2. Crayon Shin-chan - Shin

Shin is a drama that revolves around the daily story between the family members of a five-year-old boy - Shin, who was born into an ordinary family in Japan.

Shin and his parents, younger sister and friends created funny situations. With his innocent, childlike personality, how will the boy solve the troubles caused by himself? Through this film, parents will also partly understand how their children behave and have better ways to handle them.

3. Hotaru no Haka

Hotaru no Haka is a film adaptation of a popular novel, about a touching brotherhood. The film is set in Japan at the end of World War II, revolving around two main characters Seita and Setsuko - poor orphans.

The two brothers witnessed their mother's death because the US government dropped a bomb on the city of Kobe. It is even more painful when the two children have to endure the cold and unfair treatment of their aunt and face a devastating famine. How will both brothers do, how will they make a decision?

4. My Neighbor Totoro

My Neighbor Totoro is an anime film about the love between Satsuki and Mei with her mother. The film is also a story revolving around Satsuki and Mei with a strange animal Totoro.

The family of two sisters Satsuki and Mei for some reason had to move to a new house. Here, they made new friends. Mei in a random walk in the forest met the lord of the forest and she named it Totoro.

Later, Satsuki also accidentally met Totoro once. An unexpected event occurred when the two girls' mother became ill and could not return home as promised to the two girls. So with Totoro's help, Mei and Satsuki secretly visit their mother. Will the mother of two children's condition improve to be discharged in time to meet the two sisters?

5. Wolf Children

Wolf Children is a Japanese anime film about mother and child love. The film is a story revolving around the life of a young single mother and her two half-human, half-wolf children, Ame and Yuki.

Hanna, a Japanese schoolgirl, has met and fell in love with a man who is a werewolf. She later gave birth to two children: a boy named Amre and a daughter named Yuki. Unfortunately, while looking for food, the children's father had an accident and died.

What does Hanna have to do to take care of and raise these two special children? Will they avoid the eyes of those around them to live a happy life?
